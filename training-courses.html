COURSE INFORMATION

The following applies to courses held at our own training facilities:
Course Duration

Courses are run from Monday to Friday, from 08h30 to 15h30.

Cellphones

In order to ensure that learners benefit fully from each course they take, the use of cell phones is restricted during the course or workshop.

Lunch and Refreshments

Lunch is provided between 12h30 and 13h30, with a choice of venue, and refreshments are served at 10h00 and 14h00.
