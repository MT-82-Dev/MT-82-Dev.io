Advantages

Class Sizes

We limit the size of our classes to a maximum of 8 learners at Keybase, to ensure that all our learners receive personal attention and individual support.

Support

Keybase provides free telephonic assistance to all learners, specific to the courses attended. Additional on-site support is available.

Course Material

We provide comprehensive, easy to use course documentation, including instruction notes and exercises focused on the course material to encourage both theoretical and practical learning.

Expert Facilitators

Our facilitators are highly experienced, with the ability to adapt and relate to the requirements of our learners. Each facilitator holds relevant certification where appropriate.

Services

In-house Training

In-House Training

Training is conducted at our comfortable, air-conditioned facilities in Newlands, Pretoria. All stationery, refreshments, lunch and workstations are provided by us.

-Maximum of eight learners-

On-Site Training

On-Site Training

Training is conducted at your premises for your convenience. We provide the stationery and manuals. You will need to provide the rest; including a workstation for each learner.

-Maximum of ten learners-

Consulting

Consulting Services

We offer the service of creating and editing databases, spreadsheets, documents and presentations for you.

Assessments

Assessments Services

Summative assessments of learning refer to the evaluation of participants where the focus is on the outcome of a program. This contrasts with formative assessment (Class Activities), which summarizes the participants' development at a particular time.
